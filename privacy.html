Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Legal jargon
TL;DR (Plain English)

1 Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We do not access any other Google account data. No passwords are stored on our side.

2 How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your session and display your name and avatar in the app.
  • To associate your workspace data (projects, tasks, notes, teams) with your account.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties.

3 Data Storage & Security

Most of your app data — projects, tasks, notes, team members, preferences — is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This architecture ensures your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers without your explicit action.

Your authentication state is securely managed by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC), utilizing secure HTTP-only session cookies and industry-standard encryption protocols.

4 Cookies & Tracking

We respect your digital footprint. We use a Firebase session cookie solely for maintaining your logged-in state. We do not use advertising, tracking, or cross-site analytics cookies.

5 Your Rights & Controls

  • Sign Out: You can terminate your session at any time from the app's Settings page.
  • Data Clearing: You hold full control over your local data. You can erase it entirely by clearing your browser's local storage data for this site.
  • Account Deletion: You can request full account and metadata deletion by emailing privacy@byteforgenet.dev.

6 Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as our platform evolves. Significant changes will be explicitly communicated within the app interface. Continued use of Byteforgenet after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

7 Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please reach out securely to our privacy team at privacy@byteforgenet.dev.
