Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. We do not access any other Google account data. No passwords are stored on our side.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your session and display your name and avatar in the app
  • To associate your workspace data (projects, tasks, notes, teams) with your account
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your information with any third parties

3. Data Storage

Most of your app data — projects, tasks, notes, team members, preferences — is stored in your browser's localStorage. This means your data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

Your authentication state is managed by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC), which uses secure HTTP-only session cookies.

4. Cookies

We use a Firebase session cookie solely for maintaining your logged-in state. We do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies.

5. Your Rights

  • You can sign out at any time from the Settings page
  • You can clear all local data by clearing your browser's localStorage
  • You can request account deletion by emailing privacy@byteforgenet.dev

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated in the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@byteforgenet.dev.
