Security

Your security is our priority

We take a thoughtful approach to protecting your account and data from unauthorized access.

🔐

Firebase Auth

Authentication is powered by Google Firebase — enterprise-grade, battle-tested, and zero-password on our side.

🏠

Local-First Data

Your projects, tasks, and notes live in your browser's localStorage. We never transmit or store them on our servers.

🛡️

Role Permissions

Team access is governed by a role system — Owner, Admin, Member — with granular permission levels per action.

Authentication Security

  • All sign-ins use Google OAuth 2.0 — we never see or store your password
  • Firebase session tokens are managed securely through browser-local persistence
  • Unauthenticated requests to protected pages are automatically redirected to login

Data Security

  • App data (tasks, notes, teams) is stored only in your device's localStorage
  • No personal data is transmitted to external servers other than Google Firebase
  • All network requests use HTTPS
  • Clearing your browser's localStorage removes all app data from your device

Report a Vulnerability

If you've discovered a security issue, please disclose it responsibly. Do not publicly post vulnerability details before we've had a chance to address them.

🔍 Report to security@byteforgenet.dev
